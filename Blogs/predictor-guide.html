Using CollegeMitra College Predictor Find Your Perfect Engineering College with CollegeMitra College Predictor: An Easy and Effective Guide, it is an easy and effective way to find the right engineering college for you. Here's how you can use it effectively.

1. Set your JEE Mains rank

To start, you will need to enter your JEE Mains rank. This is a critical factor in determining your college options, so be sure to enter your rank accurately.

2. Select your category

CollegeMitra College Predictor offers several categories to choose from, including General, OBC, SC, ST, and others. Choose the category that best represents your eligibility criteria.

3. Select your gender

If you are a female candidate, you can choose either the "Gender-neutral" or "Female only" option. If you are male, you will only be able to select "Gender-neutral."

4. Choose your counseling

You can select either HSTES, UPTU, CSAB, or "All" in CSAB counselling. Your category will be applied based on your selection, but in UPTU and MPDTE you will be considered an open category if you are not from the home state.

5. View your results

Once you have entered all of the necessary information, simply click the "Predict" button to view your results. CollegeMitra College Predictor will generate a list of colleges that match your eligibility criteria based on your JEE Mains rank, category, and other important factors.
